Our Attractions

Climbing Walls

Take climbing to the next level with the ultimate vertical obstacle course. Climb, jump, and fly across a challenging course!

Foam Zone

Push. Evolve. Fly. The Foam Zone is all about big air and soft landings. Fly or flip into a pool of huge foam squares.

Ninja Course

Enter a jumper. Exit a Ninja. For athletes looking to push themselves, the Ninja Course will put your abilities to the test.

Toddler Zone

Toddler Zone is an exclusive area for toddlers and parents to laugh and play. A time for your little one to discover the joy of jumping.

Obstacle Course

Ready to level up? Test your parkour skills to the next level. Train your speed, power, and strength by practicing on hurdles placed in your path.

Flying Hoops

We're throwing a party above the rim, and everyone's invited. Come push the limits of what's possible.

Dodgeball Zone

The funnest sport in PE plus trampolines? Yes, please. All ages are welcome and everyone loves the experience.

Café

Need to refuel? Park your go-karts, holster your lasers, and slide right over to the The Rush Cafe. Then get right back to activating awesome

Battle Beam

Think balance beam plus sword fighting. Only the swords are foamy. And the pit below is lined with cushy blocks.
